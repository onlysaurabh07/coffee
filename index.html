THE JOURNEY

Farm

FROM SOIL
TO CUP

We source exclusively from altitude farms above 1,500 metres in Ethiopia, Colombia and Java. Our green buyers spend weeks living alongside farmers, understanding not just the bean — but the soul behind the harvest.

Every lot is numbered, dated, and tasted blind before it crosses our threshold. Traceability isn't a feature. It's our foundation.

FARM
Roast

FIRE &
PRECISION

Our roastery runs a Vienna-built drum roaster circa 1962, rebuilt with modern sensors that log temperature every 250 milliseconds. The data tells us when to push. Intuition tells us when to hold.

We roast in micro-batches of 12kg. Never more. Never compromised.

FARM TO CUP

01 — Cultivation
High Altitude Farms

Hand-picked cherries from 1,500m+ elevation. Shade grown, naturally processed.

02 — Processing
Washed & Natural

Two-stage fermentation reveals complex fruit notes. Dried on raised African beds for 21 days.

03 — Roasting
Micro-Batch Precision

12kg drum roasts. Data-logged every 250ms. Rested 72 hours before release.

04 — Your Cup
The Final Ritual

Brewed at 93°C to 92 bar. A 28-second extraction. Nothing more. Nothing less.
